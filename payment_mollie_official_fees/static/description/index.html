Effortlessly manage transaction fees for Mollie e-commerce payments

How to configure

Step 1 Download the module and place the module in apps folder.

Step 2 Install this module in your Odoo instance.

Step 3 Open Website > Configuration > Payment Providers and open mollie provider.

Step 4 Select product for the fees.

Step 5 Click on Mollie Payment Methods tab and click on Enable Payment Methods.

Step 6 Open the payment method to configure the fees .

DONE That's it you are ready to accept fees for mollie payments.
